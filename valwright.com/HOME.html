Val wright watercolors

 
 

My love is painting outdoors, capturing the moment when it is fresh and crisp. The realistic setting becomes less important than the poetic movement of nature.


As I see and hear the movement I let my brush join the dance, pirouetting from one form to another.


Transparent watercolors are most effective at implying there is an underlying force controlling the painting, whether it is the dark shadows of a lilly pond or a hot blast from the sun.
